This webpage is intented to share information about Como Park Zoo and Conservatory. Important Disclaimer: The webmaster does not own Como Park Zoo or Conservatory or any of its products, information or images. At Como Zoo and Conservatory there are many animals to observe from all over the world. Additionally there is a large variety of plants to view in the indoor conservatory. Although it is free to visit Como Park, a small donation is requested.

Some of the Activities Available at Como are as fallows

  • Animals from around the world
  • Conservatory with a large of variety of plansts from around the world
  • Rides for Children and Families
  • Educational courses and school field trips
  • Places to host special events such as weddings, picnics, birthdays, shows and more!
